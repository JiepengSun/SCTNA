About Us

The Trojans Neighborhood Association, TNA, is a student organization at the University of Southern California founded in 2018. USC is located in South Los Angeles. The unique inner-city location comes with challenges but also offers opportunities for students to engage with the community to improve quality of life. The Trojans Neighborhood Association aims to bring together students and community members in order to fight the “wicked problems” our neighborhood faces. “Wicked problems” refer to complex issues that are resistant to resolution. They include poverty; food and water security; social justice issues; public health issues; sustainability and climate change; homelessness; renters’ rights; aging; and many more. Our goal is to bring awareness to these issues and to work toward solutions through projects and workshops. TNA operates within the USC DPS monitored zone. Currently, we are focusing primarily on the area surrounding the University Park Campus, but we hope to expand our influence to the area surrounding the Health Sciences Campus in the future.
